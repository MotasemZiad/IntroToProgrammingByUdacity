Tiger

Tigers are exceptional swimmers and enjoy spending time in the water, making them one of the few cat species with this ability.

  • Scientific Name: Panthera tigris
  • Average Length: 9 feet
  • Average Lifespan: 10-15 years
  • Habitat: Forests, grasslands, mangroves, and snowy regions.

They are the largest of all big cats, with adult males weighing up to 500 pounds (227 kilograms) and measuring around 9 feet (2.7 meters) in length, excluding the tail. Female tigers are slightly smaller but still impressive in size. Tigers have muscular bodies, a long tail for balance, and strong jaws equipped with sharp teeth and retractable claws.
